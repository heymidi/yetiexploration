ABOUT

Yeti Exploration is a versatile provider of early-stage mineral exploration field services and geotechnical data collection. Our specialties are in acquisition of mineral titles and property evaluation.

Our crews have well balanced skillsets and are accustomed to working in many different terrains. We are always mindful of the impact of our work on local environments and communities, and strive for respect and efficiency throughout every project.

While our primary focus is in Yukon and British Columbia, we can provide services across Canada.

SERVICES

Claim Staking:

Acquisition of mineral titles for mining companies and individuals. Our experienced crew will take all measurementsto ensure your claims are staked to government and industry standards.

Geophysics:

Ground magnetics. Data is carefully collected and transferred to your geophysicist.

Drill Pad Construction:

Quality platforms in varied landscapes.

Geochemistry:

Soils, regional silts, horizon, rocks. All crew members are trained in proper sampling procedures and handling. Utmost care is taken to avoid contamination throughout the process.

Grid Set-Up and Line-Cutting:

We push for accuracy on your grids and ease of movement for your personnel through various terrains.

Camp Building:

Construction of standard exploration camps. The aim is practical functionality and minimal disturbance.
